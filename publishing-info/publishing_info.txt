## Full skill discription

Vitamin guide a simple skill which tells you about vitamins, their sources and why they are important for your body. 

It has all the 13 vitamins. 
Namely, Vitamin A, B1, B2, B3, B5, B6, B7, B9, B12, C, D, E, and K.

Usage :
Enable the skill, then invoke by saying -
"Alexa, open vitamin guide",
then simply start by asking about a vitamin, say -
"Tell me about Vitamin C", or just say "Vitamin C"

Say "help" to know more about the skill.

* compatible with echo dot, echo show and other alexa devices with screen. 

Disclaimer -
This skill does not provide medical advice, and is for informational and educational purposes only, 
and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, treatment or diagnosis. 

## example phrases

Alexa, open Vitamin Guide
